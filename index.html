About KenyansDoCare

KenyansDoCare is a non-profit organisation seeking to address issues corruption, and social inequality, leading to low standards of living.
This will be achievable by exposing the suspected corruption incidences to help the judicial system in following up and acting accordingly.
We believe that every individual deserves equal opportunities and resources.

Our Vision

KenyansDoCare aims to create a more equitable, just, and compassionate society by working towards the following objectives:

  1. Exposing any suspected person spanning from Politicians, Businessmen, Police Officers, and any other civil servant misusing public resources for personal gains
  2. Enhancing Justice by questioning the relevant authorities like DCI Kenya, NIS, Police Services, and all relevant agencies about the actions taken against unfair activities in our nation
  3. Improving accountability of civil servant leaders
  4. Improving access to basic services and education
  5. Eradicating Corruption
  6. Promoting health and well-being among the population
  7. Strengthening social and economic ties
  8. Addressing poverty and inequality
